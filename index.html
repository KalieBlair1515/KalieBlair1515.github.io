The Mystical Forest

Inside a mystical forest lives:

  • fairies
  • gnomes
  • talking bunnies

Every day the fairies wake up to a daily routine of:

  1. eating breakfast
  2. singing with birds
  3. playing with the bunnies
  4. eating lunch
  5. spread fairy dust on flowers
  6. eat dinner
  7. watch the sunset
  8. do night time yoga
  9. go to bed
